Petget: Core

The foundation of a multi-species breeding management platform on Odoo.

What this module does

Petget Core gives you one central, species-agnostic profile for every animal in your program, plus the everyday tools breeders and kennels actually use.

  • Animal profile — auto System ID, call & registered name, sex, date of birth (with computed age), colour, microchip, registration number, status, current owner, sire/dam pedigree links, and a photo.
  • Documents — upload certificates and images per animal with category and metadata; stored privately (authenticated access only).
  • Reminders — vaccination, worming, heat, vet visit, grooming; Pending/Completed with automatic Overdue and Due-Today views.
  • Activity timeline — typed notes (General / Care / Health / Breeding / Follow-up) with author and date.
  • Built-in chatter & activities — every record has Odoo's message log and scheduled-activity reminders.
