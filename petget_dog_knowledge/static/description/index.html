Petget: Dog Knowledge Base

Breed knowledge, life stages, feeding & reproduction guidance.

What this module does

  • Breed profiles — origin, temperament, size/weight/height, lifespan, grooming/exercise/trainability, common health issues, and a breed photo.
  • Reproduction guidance — first-heat age, heat-cycle interval, gestation, and average litter size per breed.
  • Canine life stages — a shared reference (neonatal → senior) with feeding/nutrition and care guidance for each stage.
  • Per-dog development — every dog shows its current life stage and the matching feeding guidance, computed from its date of birth.
  • Detailed data pre-loaded for popular breeds; the rest are easy to fill in.

Requires the free Petget: Dog module. Breed photos are not bundled (licensing) — upload your own. Health/nutrition notes are general guidance; always consult your veterinarian.
