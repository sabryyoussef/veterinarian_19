# Logo / Visual Identity Brief — Pet Spot

## Recommended direction
A clean veterinary + pet care identity, not a childish pet shop logo. The strongest commercial direction is: premium but friendly, medical trust + summer North Coast lifestyle.

## Suggested logo concept
- Icon: paw + medical cross or stethoscope line forming a pet face.
- Text: “Pet Spot” as the main English name.
- Arabic support line: “بيت سبوت” or “عيادة بيت سبوت البيطرية”.
- Tagline options:
  - Your Pet’s Summer Care Spot
  - Clinic • Grooming • Boarding • Petshop
  - رعاية أليفك في الساحل

## Recommended colors
- Deep navy / charcoal for trust.
- Aqua/turquoise accent for North Coast summer.
- Warm coral/orange accent for friendliness and visibility.
- White background for clean medical look.

## Required logo exports
- Square logo for Facebook/Instagram profile.
- Horizontal logo for cover/post headers.
- White transparent logo for photo overlays.
- One-color black logo for invoices, uniforms, and signage.

## Avoid
- Overcrowded dog/cat/vet icons.
- Too many colors.
- Generic stock-paw logo with no medical identity.
