# Pet Spot Marketing Campaign Brief

## Campaign objective
Generate bookings for North Coast seasonal pet care: clinic visits, vaccinations, grooming, boarding, and home/mobile visits.

## Primary audience
- North Coast vacation residents with cats/dogs.
- Pet owners who need trusted care while on holiday.
- Existing old customer database from previous seasons.

## Core campaign message
Pet Spot is the trusted North Coast pet care destination for summer: vet clinic, grooming, boarding, and petshop in one place.

## Suggested campaign angles
1. Summer opening / we are back in Sahel.
2. Vaccination and checkup before vacation.
3. Grooming day before beach season.
4. Safe boarding while the family is out.
5. Emergency vet care nearby.
6. Mobile/home visit for compounds.

## Suggested Arabic copy pillars
- “رجعنا الساحل — رعاية أليفك قريبة منك.”
- “كشف، تطعيم، جروومينج، بوردينج — كل احتياجات أليفك في مكان واحد.”
- “اطمن على أليفك وانت في الإجازة.”
- “عيادة بيطرية في الساحل تحت إشراف متخصص.”

## Suggested English copy pillars
- “Your pet’s care spot in Sahel.”
- “Vet care, grooming, boarding, and petshop — all in one place.”
- “Safe summer care for cats, dogs, and exotic pets.”

## Initial service price hooks from user-provided business context
- Checkup: 1000 EGP
- Vaccinations: 1000 EGP
- Grooming: 1500 EGP
- Boarding hourly: 250 EGP/hour
- Boarding full day: 500 EGP/day
- Home visit: 1000 EGP + transportation fee around 500–1000 EGP

## Recommended first 10 posts
1. Opening announcement: Pet Spot is back in Sahel.
2. Storefront/location post using Google Maps/store photo.
3. Services carousel: clinic, vaccines, grooming, boarding, petshop.
4. Boarding trust post: safety, supervision, daily care.
5. Grooming before/after post.
6. Vaccination reminder post.
7. Mobile/home visit post for compounds.
8. FAQ: what to bring for boarding.
9. Customer database WhatsApp broadcast announcement.
10. Limited opening week offer / free quick check with booking if approved.
