# Manual Export Steps for Blocked Photos

## Facebook
1. Open: https://www.facebook.com/animalcarecenterpetspots/
2. Go to Photos.
3. Download or screenshot:
   - Profile photo / logo
   - Cover photo
   - Storefront/signage photos
   - Doctor/team photos
   - Grooming/boarding photos
   - North Coast opening posts
4. Save files into: 02_photos_from_links/facebook/

## Google Maps
1. Open the supplied Google Maps share link in Chrome.
2. Click the business profile photos.
3. Save/screenshot:
   - Storefront outside photo
   - Signage photo
   - Interior photo
   - Any customer review photos
4. Save files into: 02_photos_from_links/google_maps/
